about us

Welcome to our place! At Akshar,

we take pride in offering top-notch 3D printing services and innovative engineering solutions. Our mission is to revolutionize the world of manufacturing and design by harnessing the power of cutting-edge 3D printing technology. Akshar was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between creativity and reality. We understand that the possibilities of 3D printing are limitless, and we are dedicated to making those possibilities accessible to everyone. With a skilled engineers, designers, and 3D printing enthusiasts, we are committed to delivering high-quality products and exceptional service to our valued customers. Currently, we excel in working with PLA material, a biodegradable and eco-friendly thermoplastic that suits various applications. However, we are not limited to PLA and constantly strive to enhance our material offerings. We are continuously expanding our capabilities to include a wide array of materials such as ABS, PETG, nylon, and even high-performance materials like PEEK and ULTEM. If you have a project that requires 3D printing or need engineering expertise, feel free to get in touch with us. Our dedicated team is always ready to discuss your ideas, provide guidance, and deliver exceptional results. You can reach out to us through our contact page or directly via email or phone. We value your vision, and together, we can bring your creations to life. Thank you for visiting our website and considering Akshar as your trusted partner for 3D printing services and engineering solutions. We look forward to collaborating with you and turning your imagination into reality. Let's embark on a journey of innovation and creativity together!
